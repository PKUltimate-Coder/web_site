About the Company

NG IT Solutions is located in Roodepoort-Johannesburg(Gauteng), and we have striven to service all parts of Gauteng, keeping in mind that our drivers for success are that we need to maintain:

  • True collaboration with customers and partners
  • Complete understanding of the customer’s business
  • Persistence in completing the job, whatever it takes

Additionally, NG IT Solutions is gaining momentum in the delivery, installation and service support of computer systems and networks providing back-up for customer systems in order to minimize the effects of possible faults.

NG IT Solutions is a company which intends to be in front of the client’s needs, deeply understanding clients’ business and delivering and optimizing IT solutions from the initial phase of scoping the requirements, up to the final delivery.

Vision

Our vision is to make sure that we present ourselves in the most productive manner possible, consider the services we provide, funding available, organizational capabilities and established priorities, and remain consistently relevant to technology usage.

Mission

Our Mission is centered at ensuring that we meet our customer needs by creating a seamless ICT infrastructure.

  • Providing high quality hardware fixing mechanisms, professional consulting and development outsourcing that would improve our customer operations.
  • Making access to information easier and more secure (Enterprise Business)
  • Improving communication and data exchange (Business to Business)
  • Providing our customers with a Value added service
